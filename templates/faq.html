AfriSolve is a premier digital marketplace connecting Ghanaian businesses and individuals with top-tier technical talent to solve real-world problems through custom software development.

Posters list a problem or project they need solved. Developers review open problems and submit proposals (applications). The Poster reviews applications, selects a Developer, and the project moves to the active phase where progress is tracked and communication happens directly on the platform.

Yes, joining AfriSolve is completely free for both Posters and Developers. We only take a small percentage fee upon successful completion and payment of a project.

When a developer applies to your project, you can review their proposed approach, estimated timeline, and past project portfolio on their profile. We recommend chatting with them using our built-in messaging system before making a final decision.

Payments are securely held in escrow once a developer is assigned to your project. The funds are only released to the developer once you approve the final deliverables, ensuring your money is protected.

If the delivered work does not meet the agreed-upon requirements, you can request revisions. If a dispute arises, our support team will step in to mediate and determine if a refund is appropriate based on the project scope.

Navigate to the "Problems" board, find a project that matches your skills, and click "Apply". You will need to submit an estimated duration and a brief proposal explaining how you intend to solve the problem.

Once you complete the project and the Poster approves the final deliverables, the funds held in escrow are automatically released to your connected bank account or mobile money wallet within 2-3 business days.
